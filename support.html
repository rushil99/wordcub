Support

We want your experience with Word Cub to be smooth and enjoyable. If something is not working the way you expect, or you just have a question, this page should help. We are a small independent team and we genuinely read every message we receive.

Frequently asked questions

How do I play Word Cub?

Guess the hidden word by entering letters. Each guess tells you which letters are correct, which are in the word but in the wrong spot, and which do not appear at all. Keep guessing until you crack it.

My progress or streak reset — what happened?

Your scores and streaks are saved locally on your device. This can reset if you delete and reinstall the app, restore your device from a backup, or switch to a new device. Unfortunately we are unable to recover local progress on your behalf, as we do not store it on our end.

The app crashed or is not loading.

Try closing the app fully and reopening it. If the problem persists, check that your device is running a supported version of iOS and that Word Cub is up to date in the App Store. A clean reinstall resolves most persistent issues.

I found a word that seems wrong or missing.

We work hard to keep our word lists accurate and appropriate for all ages, but we know there will be gaps and the occasional head-scratcher. Please send us a note with the word in question and we will look into it.

Reporting a bug

If you have run into something that looks like a bug, we would love to hear about it. When you write in, it helps to include:

  • What you were doing when the problem occurred
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The version of Word Cub you are running (found in Settings → General → iPhone Storage)

The more detail you can share, the faster we can track it down.
